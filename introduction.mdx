## Transform your wellness journey

Vitu is a comprehensive wellness app that helps you build and maintain healthy habits across four essential pillars of wellbeing. Track your nutrition with AI-powered food analysis, monitor your daily activity, stay hydrated, and optimize your sleep - all in one beautifully designed app.

## Who is Vitu for?

Vitu is designed for anyone looking to take control of their health and wellness:

- **Health-conscious individuals** who want a unified view of their daily wellness habits
- **Fitness enthusiasts** tracking nutrition alongside exercise and recovery
- **People starting their wellness journey** who need simple, automated tracking
- **Busy professionals** who want effortless health monitoring without manual logging

## Key benefits

<Note>
  All your health data stays on your device. Vitu uses local storage (Hive NoSQL) for complete privacy and offline functionality.
</Note>

- **AI-powered insights** - Get instant nutritional analysis and personalized recipe suggestions using Google Gemini AI
- **100% local data** - Your personal health information never leaves your device
- **Automatic tracking** - Sleep and exercise detection work in the background
- **Personalized goals** - Dynamic hydration targets based on your age, weight, and gender
- **Beautiful customization** - Choose between light and dark themes, custom accent colors, and font families
- **Cross-platform** - Available on Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, and macOS

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up with your email and complete your profile with basic information (age, weight, height, gender) for personalized recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the four pillars">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Start tracking">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your progress">
    Check weekly charts and insights to see how you're progressing toward your wellness goals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology highlights

<Info>
  Vitu is built with Flutter for native performance across all platforms, using Material 3 design for a modern, intuitive interface.
</Info>

- **Google Gemini AI** for food image analysis and recipe recommendations
- **Local-first architecture** with Hive NoSQL database
- **Sensor integration** using accelerometer and GPS for activity tracking
- **fl_chart** for beautiful, interactive data visualizations
- **Material 3** design system for consistent, accessible UI
