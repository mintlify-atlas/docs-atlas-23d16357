## Overview

Vitu's Hydration feature helps you stay properly hydrated by tracking your daily water consumption. The app calculates a personalized daily goal based on your weight, age, and gender, then displays your progress in a visual circular indicator and weekly chart.

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Your Daily Goal">
    When you open the Hydration screen, you'll see:
    - A circular progress indicator showing current liters consumed vs. your goal
    - Your personalized daily target (e.g., "de 2.5 L")
    
    The goal is calculated automatically as approximately **35 ml per kg of body weight**, with adjustments for age and gender.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log Water Intake">
    Tap one of the three quick-add buttons to log water:
    - **+100 ml**: Small glass or cup
    - **+250 ml**: Standard glass (most common)
    - **+500 ml**: Large bottle or tumbler
    
    The progress circle updates immediately with a smooth animation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Your Progress">
    Watch your circular progress fill up throughout the day. When you reach 100% of your goal, the circle will be completely filled.
    
    Your total is displayed in liters (e.g., "1.5 L de 2.5 L").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Weekly Trends">
    Scroll down to see the "Consumo semanal" (weekly consumption) chart, which shows your hydration percentage for the past 7 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Data is Tracked

### Daily Metrics

- **Total ml consumed**: Sum of all water logged today
- **Daily goal (meta)**: Your personalized target in milliliters (shown in liters on screen)
- **Percentage progress**: (total_ml / goal_ml) × 100, capped at 100%

Each time you tap a button (+100ml, +250ml, +500ml), the app:
1. Adds the amount to today's total
2. Saves a timestamped log entry
3. Updates the daily summary in local storage
4. Recalculates your percentage

### Weekly History

Vitu stores your hydration data for the **past 7 days**, showing percentage achieved each day in a line chart.

<Info>
  At the start of each new day (midnight), your consumption automatically resets to **0%**. Previous days' data is preserved in the weekly chart.
</Info>

## How Your Daily Goal is Calculated

Vitu uses a smart formula to determine your ideal daily water intake:

### Base Formula
```
Base Goal = Body Weight (kg) × 35 ml
```

### Age Adjustments
- **Under 14 years old**: Goal reduced to 90% of base (children need less)
- **65 years or older**: Goal reduced to 95% of base (elderly need slightly less)
- **Ages 14-64**: Full base amount

### Gender Adjustments
- **Male (Masculino)**: +200 ml added to the goal
- **Female (Femenino)**: No additional adjustment

### Goal Limits
The final goal is clamped between:
- **Minimum**: 1,200 ml (1.2 L)
- **Maximum**: 4,500 ml (4.5 L)

<Tip>
  Example: A 30-year-old male weighing 70 kg would have a goal of:
  
  (70 kg × 35 ml) + 200 ml = 2,650 ml ≈ **2.7 L per day**
</Tip>

## How Results Are Displayed

### Circular Progress Indicator

The main visual on the Hydration screen is a large animated circle showing:
- **Current consumption** in liters (e.g., "1.2 L")
- **Goal** in smaller text below (e.g., "de 2.5 L")
- **Fill percentage** visually represented by the colored arc
- Smooth animation when you add water

The circle uses your app's theme color and fills clockwise as you drink more.

### Quick-Add Buttons

Three horizontal buttons below the circle:
- Each shows a water drop icon
- Labels: "+100 ml", "+250 ml", "+500 ml"
- Styled as elevated buttons with rounded corners
- Tap to instantly log that amount

### Weekly Line Chart

The "Consumo semanal" section displays:
- **7-day trend line** showing percentage achieved each day
- **X-axis labels**: L, M, X, J, V, S, D (Monday through Sunday)
- **Y-axis labels**: 0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%
- **Blue line** (your theme color) with dots at each day
- **Shaded area** under the line for visual emphasis

<Note>
  The chart shows **percentage** (not absolute liters) so you can compare days even if your goal changes (e.g., if you update your weight).
</Note>

### Goal Summary Card

At the bottom, a card shows:
- **Flag icon**: "Meta diaria: X.X L" (your daily target)
- **Alarm icon**: "Recordatorio cada 2 h" with a "Configurar" button (placeholder for future reminder feature)

## Tips for Best Results

### Log Immediately After Drinking

For the most accurate tracking:
- Tap the appropriate button right after you finish a glass
- If you drank from a bottle, estimate the closest amount (e.g., a typical water bottle is 500 ml)

### Use Consistent Glassware

Get to know your common containers:
- Small coffee cup: ~100-150 ml
- Standard drinking glass: ~200-250 ml
- Large tumbler: ~400-500 ml
- Sports bottle: ~500-750 ml

<Tip>
  If you drink a full 1-liter bottle, tap "+500 ml" twice to log 1,000 ml total.
</Tip>

### Check Your Progress Multiple Times

Glance at the Hydration screen throughout the day to:
- See if you're on track (aim for ~30-40% by lunch, ~70% by dinner)
- Remind yourself to drink more if the circle is too low
- Celebrate when you reach 100%!

### Adjust for Exercise or Heat

Your base goal is a starting point. Drink more if:
- You exercised heavily (see your [Exercise](/features/exercise) step count)
- It's very hot outside
- You're feeling thirsty or dehydrated

You can exceed 100% safely—the app will continue tracking.

### Update Your Weight

If your weight changes significantly:
1. Go to **Ajustes** (Settings)
2. Update your peso (weight)
3. Return to Hydration to see your recalculated goal

<Warning>
  Changing your weight will update your goal going forward, but **past days' data** remains calculated with the old goal.
</Warning>

## Understanding the Weekly Chart

### Reading the Trend

- **Flat line near 100%**: Excellent! You're consistently meeting your goal
- **Line hovering around 50-70%**: Room for improvement; try to drink more
- **Peaks and valleys**: Inconsistent hydration; aim for steadier daily intake
- **Line below 25%**: You're significantly under-hydrated most days

### Why Percentage (Not Liters)?

The chart shows percentage because:
- It's easier to compare days ("Did I meet my goal?")
- Your goal might change if you update your profile
- 100% is always your target, regardless of the absolute amount

### Day Labels

The 7-day chart always shows:
- **Today** as the rightmost point (Sunday if today is Sunday)
- **6 days ago** as the leftmost point
- Days of the week: L (Lunes), M (Martes), X (Miércoles), J (Jueves), V (Viernes), S (Sábado), D (Domingo)

## Troubleshooting

### Progress Circle Not Updating

If tapping buttons doesn't update the circle:
- Check that you're logged in (ensure user data is loaded)
- Close and reopen the Hydration screen
- Verify the app has storage permissions

### Weekly Chart Shows All Zeros

This happens if:
- You just started using the app (no historical data yet)
- App data was recently cleared
- You logged in with a different account

Just start logging water—data will populate over the coming days.

### Goal Seems Too High or Too Low

Double-check your profile data:
1. Go to **Ajustes** > **Datos Personales**
2. Verify your:
   - **Peso** (weight in kg)
   - **Edad** (age)
   - **Género** (gender)
3. Update if needed, then return to Hydration

If the calculated goal still seems off, remember:
- Goals are **personalized estimates**, not medical advice
- Adjust your intake based on how you feel
- Consult a doctor if you have specific hydration needs

### Reminder Not Working

The "Recordatorio cada 2 h" feature is a **placeholder** in the current version. Future updates may add push notifications to remind you to drink water.
